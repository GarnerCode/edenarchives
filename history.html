History of Eden

Birth of Creation (PAGE UNDER CONSTRUCTION)

In the beginning, Eden was created by the gods as a paradise for its denizens that would soon be born from the dust of the land. Guarding the newly born world were seven shardminds, each blessed with power and control over a different and specific aspect of reality. There was Luhara, Mind of Water, Tinri, Mind of Earth, Dirazu, Mind of Fire, Nubani, Mind of Air, Sumanu, Mind of Radiance, Umbra, Mind of Darkness, and Genos, Mind of Magic. The Seven Shardminds of Creation lived separately in peace, each working together with the first denizens of Eden to raise their own civilizations from nothingness and allow the newly created world to proper.
