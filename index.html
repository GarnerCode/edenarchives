Archives of Eden

An archive of our D&D misadventures

Latest Session

The Green Eyes have made it to Castle High Moor, and inside they found the lost souls of the fallen kingdom, as well as records on the defeat and banishment of the six shardminds, in what is referred to as the Abysal Plague, committed by Genos and Lord Kaiser together. While searching the records, one of the lost souls tells of the Trial of Darkness, a series of five tests created to defeat the five senses and allow for mastery over darkness, and access to the Shard of Umbra, a piece of the Mind of Darkness that has protected the castle from the spellstorms created by Genos for centuries.
